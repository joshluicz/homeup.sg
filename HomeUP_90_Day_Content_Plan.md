## Part 3: UTM Naming Convention

**Structure:** `?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=article&utm_campaign=[article-slug]`

Apply this to every WhatsApp CTA link inside blog articles. The WhatsApp link becomes:
`https://wa.me/6580877015?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=article&utm_campaign=[slug]`

Note for developer: WhatsApp links don't natively pass UTM data into WhatsApp — you need to track the click before redirect. Set up a redirect via a tracking layer (e.g., a short link through Bitly, or a server-side redirect page on homeup.sg that logs the UTM before forwarding to WhatsApp). ## Part 4: 30-Day Measurement Framework

### What to track (set up before publishing Article 1)

| Metric | Where to find it | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Organic impressions per article | Google Search Console | Shows whether Google is indexing and surfacing the article |
| Click-through rate per article | Google Search Console | Shows whether the title/meta description converts searchers to visitors |
| WhatsApp CTA click rate | Redirect tracking (see UTM note above) | The primary conversion metric |
| Checklist downloads | Email platform / form analytics | Measures top-of-funnel lead capture |
| Planning calls booked from content | Ask callers "how did you find us?" or track WhatsApp source | Closes the loop on content → revenue |

### What "working" looks like at Day 30

The plan is working if, across all 5 articles combined:
- At least 200 organic impressions in Google Search Console
- At least 3 WhatsApp CTA clicks tracked via redirect
- At least 1 confirmed planning call attributed to blog content

These are intentionally conservative. The goal at 30 days is signal, not scale.

### Decision rules at Day 30

**If 0 WhatsApp clicks from any article:** The CTA placement or copy is the problem before the traffic is. Rewrite the CTA sections on the two highest-traffic articles. Test "Book a free 30-minute planning call — no commitment" as the link text instead of generic "Book a planning call."

**If traffic exists but 0 clicks:** Readers are not connecting the article content to a next step. Add a contextual CTA mid-article that references the specific article topic (e.g., "Not sure how this applies to your CPF balance? That's exactly what a planning call covers.").

**If 0 organic impressions after 30 days:** The articles are not indexed or ranking. Check: Are pages indexed in Google Search Console? Are meta titles and descriptions set correctly? Is internal linking in place from the homeup.sg homepage to each article? Fix indexing before producing more content.

**If all metrics are zero:** The blog-first path needs reassessment. Pivot primary effort to one short-form video per week (60-second format, fixed-fee story as hook) and evaluate after 30 days of video publishing.
